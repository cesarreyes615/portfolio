Manager of Money - Case Study

Project Overview

I took a 10 week course where I worked with a team of three to create a mobile web app centered around the theme of mindfulness. We were tasked with creating an app that helped our users be more aware of a particular aspect of their lives.

Our application, Manager of Money, is a money budgeting app that is aimed at helping college students manage and budget their money better. I was mainly responsible for coding the app. I coded most of the front end and all of the back end using html, css, and javascript. I also helped come up with its features, tested prototypes, and deployed them online.

Problem Statement

College students need a way to easily keep track of their finances and budget in order to make sure they have enough money for basic necessities while also being able to practice financial management since college students typically arrive at university with little to no knowledge of money management while at the same time having a restricted supply of money.

Storyboards

Our team created three storyboards to better demonstrate how our app fulfills the needs of our users. Storyboard number one describes how our app can help save up for something the user wants. Number two demonstrates how proper budgeting gave a user some left over money to purchase a game they wanted. Number three shows an example of how helpful notifications of upcoming payments would be.

Alternate Designs

The main alternate design that our team tested was changing the size of the help button on the log in page. We did A/B testing through google analytics to see if making the help button bigger would effect if it received more clicks. We found that the size of the button did not increase the number of clicks.
